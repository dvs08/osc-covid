About

The Overseas School of Colombo is planning a fundraiser to raise money for COVID-19 Relief in Sri Lanka through UNICEF. All funds will be used to support school children in Sri Lanka to safely return to school after the pandemic. We are planning a Zoom-a-thon to be livestreamed on YouTube on Tuesday, June 9 from 9:30 am - 12:30 pm in order to get the community involved with this great cause. The Zoom-a-thon will be a fun-filled 3 hours that aims to show the OSC community our collective spirit, creativity and unity in diversity (and adversity).

COVID-19 Relief by UNICEF Sri Lanka

All proceeds from OSC's fundraiser will be allocated by UNICEF Sri Lanka. Here's some of the work they've been doing so far to protect Sri Lankan Lives and Livelihoods amidst this pandemic.
